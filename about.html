About Our School

West Scranton Intermediate School is proud to be the home of the Invaders. We serve a diverse community of learners in grades 5 through 8, focusing on academic excellence and social-emotional growth.

Our faculty is dedicated to creating a safe and engaging environment where students can explore their interests and prepare for their transition to high school.
