About Us!

Welcome to West Scranton Intermediate School, where the spirit of the Invader shines bright! We’re thrilled to introduce you to our warm, energetic school community. Situated in Scranton, PA, our school serves students in grades 5–8, providing a supportive atmosphere for exploration and learning.

A Glimpse of Our History

West Scranton Intermediate School has long been a cornerstone of educational excellence. Our mascot, the Invader, symbolizes resilience, exploration, and continuous growth—values we carry from our past into the present.

Exciting Renovations Underway

Our school is undergoing renovations to modernize classrooms, technology, and common areas—creating a state-of-the-art learning environment for our students, set to complete in 2025.

Our Philosophy

We aim to create a nurturing, inclusive environment. Our educators respect each student’s individuality and offer diverse learning experiences to ensure every Invader feels valued and inspired.

The Invader Experience

Even during renovations, our temporary setting replicates our caring, engaging environment. We continue to offer a variety of subjects and extracurricular activities, from arts to leadership clubs.

Community Connection

We organize events and projects connecting students, families, and the wider Scranton community, fostering civic pride and engagement.

Join Our Journey

We invite everyone to join the Invader family. For more information, feel free to reach out. Together, we look forward to a future where every Invader can thrive!
