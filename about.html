About Our School

West Scranton Intermediate School is proud to serve the students and families of the West Side. Our mission is to provide a challenging academic environment that fosters personal growth, responsibility, and community pride.

As the "Home of the Invaders," we offer a variety of programs including STEM, performing arts, and competitive athletics. Our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to ensure every student in grades 5-8 is prepared for the transition to high school and beyond.
