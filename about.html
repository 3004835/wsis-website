About Our School

West Scranton Intermediate School provides a challenging and supportive environment where students can succeed. Located in the heart of West Scranton, we focus on preparing our 5th-8th graders for high school and beyond with a strong emphasis on core academics and community values.

Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to the success of every Invader, fostering a culture of respect, responsibility, and academic excellence.
