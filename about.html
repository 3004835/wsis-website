About Us!

Welcome to West Scranton Intermediate School, where the spirit of the Invader shines bright! We’re thrilled to introduce you to our warm, energetic school community. Situated in the lively city of Scranton, PA, our school serves students in grades 5 through 8, providing a supportive atmosphere where young minds are encouraged to explore, innovate, and feed their curiosity.

A Glimpse of Our History

West Scranton Intermediate School has long been a cornerstone of educational excellence in the Scranton community. Our beloved mascot, the Invader, symbolizes our collective spirit of resilience, exploration, and continuous growth—values that we carry from our rich past into our present-day educational journey.

Exciting Renovations Underway

We're embarking on a new chapter as our school undergoes a comprehensive renovation, set for completion in 2025. This transformation signifies our commitment to providing the best facilities for our young Invaders. The renovation will modernize every classroom, upgrade our technological capabilities, and reinvigorate our common areas, creating a state-of-the-art learning environment.

Our Philosophy

At West Scranton Intermediate, we're dedicated to creating a nurturing, inclusive learning environment. Our passionate educators are committed to understanding and respecting each student's individuality, offering diverse learning experiences that cater to different strengths and interests. This philosophy ensures each Invader feels valued and inspired.

The Invader Experience

While our building is transforming, our heart remains the same. Students are enjoying a temporary setting that replicates the caring, engaging environment our school is known for. We continue to offer a wide range of subjects and extracurricular activities, from arts and sports to clubs that promote leadership and community spirit.

Community Connection

Our connection to Scranton is deep and heartfelt. We regularly organize events that unite students, families, and staff with the broader community. Our local partnerships and community service projects emphasize the importance of civic engagement, instilling a sense of communal pride in our students.

Join Our Journey

We extend a warm invitation to join the Invader family. For more information or any inquiries, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us. Together, we look forward to embracing a future where every Invader can thrive!
