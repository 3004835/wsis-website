📖 About West Scranton Intermediate School

West Scranton Intermediate School, located in the heart of the Hill Section, has been a pillar of educational excellence since 1923. We serve approximately 800 students in grades five through eight, providing a rigorous and supportive environment that prepares young learners for high school and beyond.

Our mission is to cultivate intellectual curiosity, integrity, and community engagement. We offer a comprehensive curriculum including accelerated math, STEM labs, band & orchestra, world language exploration, and a thriving athletic program. Our school colors are blue and white, symbolizing loyalty and purity of purpose.

With a dedicated team of 70+ teachers and staff, we maintain small class sizes and individualized support. We pride ourselves on our inclusive culture, PBIS initiatives, and strong family partnerships. The school facility includes modern science labs, a media center, and a newly renovated gymnasium.

West Scranton Intermediate is a place where every student belongs, every achievement is celebrated, and futures are built. Go Invaders!
